OS CHESS

Dedicated to open-source chess.

This is a site containing a large list of open-source chess programs, including informative articles about each one. In the open-source spirit, you can make also requests to add and edit articles, expanding on the knowledge base. Logging in will allow you to see whether the status of your change requests.

In particular, this site has a focus on chess-playing programs and their implementation. A detail search is provided for people looking for engines.
